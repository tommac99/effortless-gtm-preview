The three signal layers

Where the right moment lives.

Borrowed from the workflows.io playbook and rebuilt on EU infrastructure. We monitor all three layers in parallel and let your highest-intent signals fire outreach first.

L01 / FIRST-PARTY

Your own data.

Things only your stack knows.

  • Website visits, page paths, return frequency
  • CRM activity and pipeline state
  • Email opens, replies, opt-outs
  • Product usage if you have it
ingest: CRM webhooks · Segment · server logs
L02 / SECOND-PARTY

Your network.

Signals from people around your accounts.

  • LinkedIn post engagement & profile views
  • Ad-platform interaction patterns
  • Newsletter / community engagement
  • Event & webinar attendance
ingest: Sales Navigator · LI APIs · ad platforms
L03 / THIRD-PARTY

The market.

Everyone else's data, watched for you.

  • Funding rounds and M&A activity
  • Hiring intent and JD changes
  • Tech-stack moves (BuiltWith deltas)
  • Exec changes and org chart shifts
ingest: Crunchbase · Clearbit · BuiltWith · custom scrapes
